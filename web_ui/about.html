8 in 10 college students experience daily stress

1 in 6 college students has been diagnosed with anxiety

So why is mental health not talked about more?

Awearness is a special scarf embedded with wearable tech. It is designed to raise awareness about stress and anxiety among college students. We think it's important for students to know that they aren't along in feeling overwhelmed. By wearing a scarf, "sending love", or simply sharing a logo, you can support the cause of mental health.

How does Awearness help?

Here are the key features.

A Connected Community

Awearness raises awareness about mental health among college students, and it connects students so they feel less alone.

Relaxing Sounds

With the quick press of a button on the scarf, Awearness plays sounds to calm down a stressed student.

Send Love

Through the website, anyone can send love to students with Awearness scarves. They will get little vibrations.
